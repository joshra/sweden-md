# Official Websites

> **30-second overview:** Official websites form Sweden.md's first source layer for policy, institutions, statistics, and public-facing state narratives. Their value lies not only in credibility, but in helping writers choose the right kind of evidence for the right kind of article.

## Why it matters

When an article touches government, parliament, public institutions, or formal policy, official websites are usually the correct starting point. They may not always provide the deepest interpretation, but they provide the most stable institutional language.

## Key sites and their uses

### government.se

Use for ministries, policy statements, institutional framing, and official government communication.

### riksdagen.se

Use for parliament, legislation, procedure, democratic structure, and formal political process.

### sweden.se

Use for internationally legible explanations of culture, society, and national self-presentation in English.

### scb.se

Use for data: population, labor, education, housing, regions, and other measurable public facts.

## How to use them well

These sites should not be attached to articles as decorative links. They should be chosen according to the question being asked: institutional structure, policy language, public statistics, or outward-facing cultural framing.
